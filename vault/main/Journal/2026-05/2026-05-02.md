## 00:02 — Bot restart root-cause investigation

- Bot received SIGTERM at 00:01:13, mid-response during an opus call started at 00:00:46
- Watchdog logs empty — watchdog was not the cause; shutdown was gracious (not a crash)
- `log show` revealed `backgroundtaskmanagementd` re-registered all 3 launch agents at 00:00:44
- Cause: likely `ClaudeBotManager.app` calling `launchctl bootstrap` on startup, triggering launchd to send SIGTERM to the running process
- Action: confirmed `launchctl kickstart -k` is the correct approach on Darwin 24+; `load/unload` deprecated
